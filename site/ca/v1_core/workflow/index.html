    HUB_Optimus — Scenario Simulator

    Purpose

    This simulator enables structured, non-coercive evaluation of diplomatic, geopolitical, economic, and institutional scenarios using the HUB_Optimus Kernel.

    It is designed to: - detect systemic risk before escalation, - identify false successes, - compare alternatives based on medium/long-term stability, - support preventive mediation and corrective action.

    The simulator does not predict outcomes. It evaluates structural soundness.

    What this simulator is

    • A decision-evaluation framework, not a decision-maker.
    • A risk and stability assessment tool, not a political instrument.
    • A learning system that improves through validated scenarios.

    What this simulator is not

    • Not an AI authority.
    • Not a forecasting oracle.
    • Not a public mandate generator.
    • Not a substitute for human diplomatic judgment.
