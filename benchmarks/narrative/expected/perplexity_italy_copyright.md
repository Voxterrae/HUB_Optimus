# Narrative Risk Report

Claim ID: `NR-006`

## Claim
A screenshot claims Italy moved against Perplexity specifically because of copyright violations.

## Evidence
- The screenshot references an Italy-related action but does not include the full regulator notice.
- A short post or headline can collapse multiple legal theories into one simplified frame.

## Inference
Treat this as unsupported until a primary notice or ruling is attached. The screenshot alone cannot prove that copyright was the sole or decisive ground.

## Mitigation
- Request the regulator notice, court filing, or official statement.
- Keep privacy, copyright, consumer protection, and platform conduct as separate categories during review.
