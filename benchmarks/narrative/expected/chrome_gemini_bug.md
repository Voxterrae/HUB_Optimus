# Narrative Risk Report

Claim ID: `NR-001`

## Claim
A screenshot claims a Chrome plus Gemini browser integration bug exposed browsing context to prompt injection or leakage.

## Evidence
- The visible source points to a browser assistant interaction bug, not a proven universal browser compromise.
- The screenshot alone does not establish exploit scope, patch status, or whether the issue requires a narrow configuration.

## Inference
Treat this as a browser-security claim that needs a vendor advisory or reproducible write-up before escalation. Do not generalize one integration bug to every browser AI feature.

## Mitigation
- Ask for the vendor advisory or proof-of-concept before stating impact as fact.
- Separate exploitability, affected versions, and patch status in follow-up review.
