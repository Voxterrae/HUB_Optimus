# Narrative Risk Report

Claim ID: `NR-003`

## Claim
A screenshot claims Persona style age verification necessarily builds a reusable biometric surveillance layer.

## Evidence
- Some age-gating flows do use selfie or liveness checks, which creates a biometric processing surface.
- The screenshot does not by itself prove retention duration, cross-service sharing, or universal deployment across providers.

## Inference
This is a mixed claim. The existence of biometric processing risk does not prove a reusable surveillance layer without policy text, retention rules, and system design details.

## Mitigation
- Check the provider privacy policy and data retention terms.
- Separate biometric collection, retention, sharing, and legal basis into distinct subclaims.
