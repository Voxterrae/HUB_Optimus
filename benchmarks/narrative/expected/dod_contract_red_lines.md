# Narrative Risk Report

Claim ID: `NR-010`

## Claim
A screenshot claims a defense contract proves full approval for autonomous offensive AI use.

## Evidence
- A procurement headline can show engagement without exposing the operative restrictions or red lines.
- The visible source does not include the full contract text, policy annex, or use-case boundaries.

## Inference
This is a mixed defense claim. Procurement can be real while the leap to offensive autonomy approval remains unproven without primary policy text.

## Mitigation
- Ask for the contract, solicitation, or official policy excerpt.
- Separate procurement, experimentation, analysis support, and autonomous weapons approval into distinct findings.
