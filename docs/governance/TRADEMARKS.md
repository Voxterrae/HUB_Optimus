# Trademarks and Name/Identity Use

HUB_Optimus is a protected identifier. This document defines permitted and prohibited uses of the name and identity.

---

## 1) Core rule

You may reference HUB_Optimus, cite it, and discuss it.

You may not present derivative systems, forks, or rebrandings as “official HUB_Optimus” without written authorization by the custodians.

---

## 2) Permitted use

Allowed:
- academic citation and commentary,
- non-commercial educational use that does not misrepresent official status,
- linking to the official repository and documentation.

---

## 3) Prohibited use

Not allowed without authorization:
- commercial branding, marketing, or paid services under the HUB_Optimus name,
- creating “official-looking” forks that diverge from the Kernel,
- confusingly similar naming intended to capture credibility.

---

## 4) Logos and visual identity

If a logo or visual identity exists, use must:
- preserve attribution,
- avoid implying endorsement for derivative products,
- follow any published brand guidelines.

---

## 5) Requests for authorization

To request authorization, contact the custodians with:
- intended use-case,
- scope (commercial/non-commercial),
- how Kernel integrity will be preserved,
- disclosure of any derivative modifications.
